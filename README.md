# Phaser
A top down retro space shooter, now playable in your browser! Available at phaser.anicable.com.

# Controls
Settings/Pause - Esc  
Movement - WASD  
Fire - Left click  
Exit Station - E  

# Enemies
Arrow - Basic enemy, follows and rams the ship.  
Zoid - Shooter enemy. Keeps its distance and shoots at the ship from afar.

# Power Ups
Shield - Blocks all damage, except black holes.

# Objects/Obstacles
Station - Upgrade your ship here. Also serves as a safe zone.  
Rings - Fly your ship through these to gain experience + a speed boost.  
Black Holes - Pretty self explanatory what these do. Stay away! Or use them to slingshot you :)
